Rad Rhino
2018/08/14

Author	Triger Mountain
Genre	Shooter

You are the last Rhino and Birb in the world, forced out of what 
little wild lands remain. Now there's nothing left to do but kill every human.
Two player shooting action! It's called Rad Rhino but player 1 is the Birb. Shoot, run, and flap!
Made in 60 some-odd hours for Ludum Dare 42 using Stephane Dallongeville's excellent Genesis SDK: https://github.com/Stephane-D/SGDK
