The Invasion
by Sly Dog Studios

In The Invasion, you must stop the space aliens from reaching your weapon, which you are in control of.
If they do, your game is over! To fend them all off successfully, fend off 14 waves of them.
Use the ‘A’ button to fire, and the D-Pad to move left or right. Get your marksmanship on!
